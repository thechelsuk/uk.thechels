Use Google Takeout to export data from your Google account. This service allows you to download a copy of your data from various Google products, such as YouTube.

1. Sign in to your Google account.
2. Select the data you want to export by checking the boxes next to the products.
3. Choose the file type and delivery method for your export.
4. Click "Create Export" to start the process.
5. Once the export is ready, you will receive a link to download your data.
6. Upload the YouTube CSV data to [Play.App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1596506190) to manage inbox.
