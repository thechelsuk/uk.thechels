1. Open Microsoft Edge and type `edge://signin-internals/` in the address bar, then press Enter.
2. Scroll down to the "Accounts" section.
3. Find the Microsoft account you want to remove.
4. Click the "Remove" button next to the account.
5. Confirm the removal when prompted.
6. Restart Microsoft Edge to ensure the changes take effect.
