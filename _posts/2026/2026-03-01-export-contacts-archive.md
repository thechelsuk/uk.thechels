## Export selected contacts

1. Go to the Contacts app  on your Mac.
2. Select one or more contacts.
3. Choose File > Export > Export vCard, choose a location, then click Save.

_The contacts are exported as a vCard file (.vcf) only._

## Export all contacts

1. Go to the Contacts app  on your Mac.
2. Choose File > Export > Contacts Archive, choose a location, then click Save.

_The contacts are exported as an archive file (.abbu) only._
