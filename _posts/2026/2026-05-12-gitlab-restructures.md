> This morning we shared with team members that we're beginning a restructuring process at GitLab, and we're running it differently than most. The planning is happening openly, including a voluntary separation window. That creates real uncertainty for our team over the next few weeks, but we believe the outcome will be better for it. Where we can, we plan to finalize the new shape of the company on or before June 1.
