Oasis is a 3D spatial audio app designed to help you sleep better, focus deeper, and find calm in the chaos. Create personalized soundscapes by layering nature sounds with binaural tones, then position them in 3D space around your head. Built on neuroscience research, Oasis uses sound to guide your brain into the mental state you need--whether that's deep sleep, laser focus, or pure relaxation.
