1. When adding the Hue Dimmer Switch to HomeKit, it will be added as a "Hue Dimmer Switch" accessory.
2. It often appears in the default room rather than the room you have selected during setup.
3. To move the Hue Dimmer Switch to the correct room, go to the Home app, find the "Hue Dimmer Switch" accessory, and tap on it.
4. Tap on the "Settings" icon (gear icon) in the top right corner.
5. Scroll down to the "Room" section and tap on it.
6. Select the correct room from the list.
