## In Browser

1. Go to <https://plus.nwslsoccer.com/> and sign-in to your account.
2. Click on the "Watch" tab to see the available games.
3. Select the game you want to watch and click on it.

## In App / On TV

1. Go to NWSL App and sign in using your account.
