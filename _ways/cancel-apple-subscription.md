ou can use your device to cancel a subscription from Apple.

1. Open the Settings app.
2. Tap your name.
3. Tap Subscriptions.
4. Tap the subscription you want to cancel.
5. Tap Cancel Subscription. You might need to scroll down to find the Cancel Subscription button.

_If there is no Cancel button or you see an expiration message in red text, the subscription is already canceled._
